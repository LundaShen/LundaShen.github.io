About Me

I have been incredibly fortunate as a country girl. I had initally lived in an ammunition depot left by the Japanese military forces in Huzhou, and then moved to Jiaxing on China’s east coast. All far away from my hometown, I have attended three institutions in three countries/regions for my B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees. I enjoy travelling and enjoy communicating with different people.

In Texas, I met my husband. We became best friends, got married, held four wedding ceremonies in China and America, and have travelled to many places together. He is a scientist super enthusiastic about research, and I enjoy discussing research topics with him.


Since 2017, while continuing my Ph.D. study, research and teaching, I have started my new journy as a mother. My two kids have absorbed a lot of my sleeping time, but they have also given me infinite strength and inspiration.


In my spare time, (when my kids were awake and around me) I love reading stories to them or exploring the nature with them, or (when my kids were not around me) I love reading, coding, and writing.
