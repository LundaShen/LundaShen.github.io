Teaching and Mentoring Experience

Both of my parents are teachers. My father is a Math teacher & retired middle school principal, and my mother is a retired Linguistics professor. I inherited their teaching philosophy, believing that (1) each student is unique and need to be treated as such, (2) encouragement gives students a lasting boost, and (3) teachers should raise the bar and work with students to see how high they can jump.


9/2014–8/2021 | Teaching Assistant / Lab Instructor | Texas A&M University

For 18 semesters, I was teaching the lab sections of courses including:

• Introductory Biology I,
• Introductory Biology II (taxonomy and evolution),
• Introductory Microbiology, and
• Integrated Human Anatomy and Physiology.
